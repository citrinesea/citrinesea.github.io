Suisei HALATION tells the story of the shortlived RST-N. It begins and ends with traditional RST order. There's a push and pull between Chiaki 🔴 and Tetora ⚫️ as center. Kanata 🔵, Midori 🟢, and Shinobu 🟡 all have a chance to be at the center to give their support, before Chiaki takes over again.

☆ Formations

3-2 and 2-3 formations are standard blocking. V-formations have two variations: four supporting the lead, or the lead supporting the four. In axis formations four people center around a single person, in an X or a checkerboard pattern.

☆ Order

Traditional RST order (🔵⚫️🔴🟡🟢) - Chiaki in the center, Kanata and Tetora on one side, Shinobu and Midori on the other. Chiaki was always the leader of RST while this order was active.

Power-up RST order (🔴🟢⚫️🟡🔵) - Post-Supervillain order, Tetora in the center, Midori and Chiaki on on side, Shinobu and Kanata on the other. Members take turns being the leader of RST.
