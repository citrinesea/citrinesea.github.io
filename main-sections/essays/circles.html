A circle centered around the chef Niki Shiina. Its members gather out of a love for cooking. They make dishes from just about every cuisine, and when what they make is more than they can eat, they act as a multinational restaurant to share that food with others.

Kaori Tea Party

A tea ceremony circle with a low threshold of entry that, while sometimes borrowing specialized tea houses to meet in, doesn’t mind gathering in more casual spaces to hold their tea parties. Sometimes, they also come together in order to temporarily forget about the hustle and bustle of everyday life and revitalize their spirits.
