AKATSUKI Charm Points and Impressions

An interview with AKATSUKI and 9 different idols about their unit!

RELEVANT CHARACTERS

Q ☾ What do you think is your unit's charm point?

Keito's Answer

AKATSUKI is a unit that focuses on traditional performing arts. After all, there is no one who can surpass us in terms of brilliance.

Many other "units" operate in a friendly manner, but unlike them, we are particular about our unique performance as an expression of our daily training and traditional culture. I think that commitment is our charm point.

Ensemble Stars Magazine Vol. 6: AKATSUKI | citrinesea

Kuro's Answer

Akatsuki is a unit with a bond stronger than blood. In other words, that's how I'd define us.

Both Kanzaki and I love to perform Akatsuki's live performances with all of us together. At first glance, we might not seem like close friends, but deep down we are connected. I think that's the most appealing thing about Akatsuki.

Personally, by belonging to Akatsuki, it's made me wanna work as an idol seriously. Right now, I can't imagine being anywhere else.

Ensemble Stars Magazine Vol. 6: AKATSUKI | citrinesea

Souma's Answer

Hasumi-dono, the leader of “Akatsuki”, is an honorable personage. Both Kiryu-dono and I rush to exert our best because of Hasumi-dono. Therefore, it can be said that the most attractive part of Akatsuki is Hasumi-dono.

A samurai has meaning only if he serves his master. But in truth, it is far more common for a samurai not to be blessed with a master. . . In that respect, I am truly blessed with a good master ♪

Ensemble Stars Magazine Vol. 6: AKATSUKI | citrinesea



Q ☾ Please tell us your impression of AKATSUKI!

Isara Mao's Answer (from Trickstar)

For a while, I had the experience of working with AKATSUKI, and not to mention, I’m also a member of the student council. I think I know more about AKATSUKI than the others.

AKATSUKI is a unit established by the vice president, and its members feel suitable for the unit. Since it incorporates traditional arts and culture, there are some aspects that are a little rigid, but it’s a good unit where you can feel a strong, deep sense of trust and unity ♪

Ensemble Stars Magazine Vol. 6: AKATSUKI | citrinesea

Fushimi Yuzuru's Answer (from fine)

The vice president is in AKATSUKI. I belong to the same archery club as the vice president, so we communicate a lot.

Mm… My impression of AKATSUKI… I have the impression that Kiryu-sama and Kanzaki-sama support the vice president, and the vice president supervises as a whole, making it a very well-balanced unit.

Ensemble Stars Magazine Vol. 6: AKATSUKI | citrinesea

Himemiya Tori's Answer (from fine)

AKATSUKI…~? fine is number one, and AKATSUKI is number two. I don't think it's necessary to say such an obvious thing now, but I’ll mention it just in case.

Well, since he's a childhood friend of the chairman, he's quite capable, and I guess it’s a unit with a sense of leadership that's different from fine.

Otogari Adonis's Answer (from UNDEAD)

AKATSUKI… It's the unit that Kanzaki belongs to. Kanzaki has been teaching me about etiquette and events in Japan.

He seems to respect Hasumi-senpai, as well as Kiryu-senpai. I think that AKATSUKI has built good interpersonal relationships with each other.

Nn… AKATSUKI? I feel like Keito was in AKATSUKI. Kuro, too! There’s some other second year as well.

Anyway, I'm focused on composing right now, so I don't care to talk about my impression of AKATSUKI! Impressions are different depending on who looks at it!

Suou Tsukasa's Answer (from Knights)

My impression of AKATSUKI…

Hmm, I belong to the same club as Hasumi-senpai, the leader of Akatsuki. It seems that Big Sis is learning how to make costumes from Kiryu-senpai. I have no contact with Kanzaki-senpai, so I can't say anything about him…

Since it has the strict Hasumi-senpai, I think it is a well-disciplined unit.

AKATSUKI… ~? Speaking of AKATSUKI, it’s a big headliner of the student council. After all, when the student council president was hospitalised for a long period of time, AKATSUKI was the student council's greatest strength.

That was how I got the impression that they're scary people, but that impression has been slowly changing. . . and now I'm not as scared now as I was before~♪
