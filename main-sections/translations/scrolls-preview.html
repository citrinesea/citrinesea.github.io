Scroll of the Elements

WRITER Akira

TRANSLATOR citrinesea PROOFREADER 310mc

Shinobu and Souma feel sympathy for one another. As ones dedicated to serving, they serve as support even in live concerts, but they are brought out into the main roles on stage by the transfer student. RYUSEITAI and AKATSUKI are to perform, at the request of a sightseeing spot. The theme is 'ninjas' and Shinobu is fired up. Souma, who has much in common with Shinobu, is also chosen for a big role, and the two of them put tremendous efforts into their leading roles.
