Reverse Crunches (ラバースクランチ)

As you exhale, pull your legs close to your body as if you’re hugging them! When you lower them, make sure to slowly flex on your core!

Bicycle Crunches (バイシクルクランチ)

Touch your elbows to your knees with these modified sit-ups! The twisting motion will strengthen your abdominal muscles while also tightening them! Keep a steady rhythm!

Twisting Crunches (ツイストクランチ)

Reach across your body, and touch near your knees with your palms! The twisting motion trains your abdominal muscles and tones them, too! Keep a steady rhythm!

Twisting Leg Raises (ツイストレッグレイズ)

Close your knees together at a right angle, and tilt your torso left and right! Effective for tightening your core!

Hip Lifts (ヒップリフト)

Lift your hips, and imagine that you’re clenching your buttocks! Get into the rhythm and burn fat!

Knee-to-Elbow (ニートゥーエルボー)

Touch your elbows and knees diagonally in front of your stomach! Tighten your core to avoid trembling! It’s effective for the whole body, especially the stomach and back muscles!

Rhythmic Back Extension (リズムバックエクステンション)

Lift your limbs diagonally, and alternate to the rhythm! Shoulder muscles and buttocks are usually tense, so let’s loosen them up!

Back Kick (バックキック)

Exhale and pull your leg towards the ceiling! Put pressure on your buttocks! It firms up your butt and burns fat too!

Split Squat (スプリットスクワット)

Touch your elbows and knees diagonally in front of your stomach! Tighten your core to avoid trembling! It’s effective for the whole body, especially the stomach and back muscles!

Squat (スクワット)

Make sure your feet are shoulder-width apart and your knees and toes are pointing in the same direction! Imagine sitting on a chair with your chest out, and push your butt out while you squat!
