PREFACE

This is a reading recommendation list for dynamics between AKATSUKI and RYUSEITAI members. Sections are divided by RST members, and subsections are Akatsuki members. I’ll give a sort of spoiler-free summary, and then the characters each story is relevant to. Translations are linked. Scroll over or click a thumbnail for story names.

Individual Dynamics Sections

Ryuseitai and Akatsuki

If you want a comprehensive look at Ryuseitai and Akatsuki as a whole, Meteor Impact, Scroll of the Elements, and Submarine are must-reads. They’re way longer than the individual character story recommendations below, but they are mandatory if you want to understand both units.

Meteor Impact |➤ Read here

Meteor Impact

In short, Meteor Impact is the origin story for pre-war Ryuseitai (Madara and Chiaki), the student council (Keito and Chiaki), Akatsuki’s role in the war (Keito, Kuro, Souma), and the reason for Kanata’s entire subjugation + Battle of the Sea God (Akatsuki v. Kanata). It also covers Kanzaki-Shinkai relations (Souma and Kanata) and why Kuro has a heavy hand in RST Live Shows (Kuro and Chiaki). The war-era part of this story ends with Chiaki and Kanata being what’s left of RST, until the 1st years (Noritama) come in the spring.

The story spans all three years of Yumenosaki !-era. There are highly sensitive themes and it’s a very heavy read, so be mindful of the disclaimers before you give it a look.



Scroll of the Elements | ➤ Read here (Wayback)

Scroll of the Elements

This is a big story for AKTK+RST post-Meteor Impact (timeline-wise), as they perform at a local tourist spot. I’ll go into some detail below when we get to what this means for some individual characters, but it’s a great story for Souma and Shinobu, and there are notable moments for Souma and RST, and Keito and Midori. It’s more slice of life-adjacent than Meteor Impact is, so it’s a lighter read!



Submarine | ➤ Read here

Submarine

Submarine takes place in !!-era, during the SS Arc. This is AKTK+RST’s third major story together, and it pulls together elements from both Meteor Impact and Scrolls. Akatsuki and Ryuseitai travel to Okinawa during the SS Tournament, and face war on the island with local idols. Because of secret orders, Akatsuki is led by Souma and Ryuseitai is led (indirectly) by Kanata. This is story reconstructs AKTK+RST’s relationship going forward

Souma

Rin's Recommendation Palace of the Ocean | ➤ Read here (Wayback)

Palace of the Ocean

Souma and Kanata are very entwined– childhood acquaintances, doomed roles to each other, etc. There’s a handful of stories that you could read to understand them in their full context. Here, I’ll recommend Palace of the Ocean. Souma becomes enamored with a turtle and spends the story taking good care of it. The last few chapters set a good baseline for Kanata and Souma’s interactions: if you read Submarine and were confused as to why Kanata is so comfortable terrorizing Souma, this is a good answer.

Other Stories

For extra RST+AKTK goodness, read Crash Course, a faux unit swap story. It’s a close second to my recommendation list, and you get to see AKTK!Kanata and RST!Souma. Colorful Autumn is good for their slice of life dynamic too.

Rin's Recommendation Four Beasts of Fistfighting | ➤ Read here (Wayback)

Four Beasts of Fistfighting

If you’re looking for a deep dive on Akatsuki lore and Tetora’s involvement in all of it, it’s here. What Supervillain!Tetora was to Chiaki is what Four Beasts!Tetora is to Kuro. This is the precedent to elevating Tetora’s senpai-worship/Kuro’s mentorship relationship. If you want to understand Tetora and Kuro at an atomic level, this is your story.

Besides that, it goes into how Tetora found RST post-Akatsuki rejection, the Dragon King Competition, and even why the Student Council broke it up during the Main Story.

Shinobu and...

Keito

Rin's Recommendation Scroll of the Elements | ➤ Read here (Wayback)

Scroll of the Elements

THIS STORY IS SO IMPORTANT TO THEM, hence its inclusion here, as opposed to a shorter story. As Shinobu tries to put together a proposal for a joint live, Keito greenlights it and maneuvers it to be a sponsored event by the Ninja Association in order to promote it. He encourages Shinobu to have pride in his club. This story establishes a strong relationship between them. After graduation, Keito enlists Shinobu as his spy for the student council to keep an eye on Mao. Chiaki and Keito discuss it further in Submarine.

Other Stories

I include Sparkly First Years for Shinobu approaching student council for the first time, though it doesn't have Keito in it. Ukiyo Air Ride is the first time they meet.
