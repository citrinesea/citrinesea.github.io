Welcome to my website! I'm Rin, a Keito-P who is way too obsessed with every aspect of RYUSEITAI, AKATSUKI and Ibuki Taki, as well as their interactions with each other.

The goal of citrinesea is to house the polished versions of my thoughts and theories I have. Additionally, I also have lore written down with its sources, so the next time I go "when did that happen?" I actually have a clean space to sift through. This is essentially a glorified notebook. Lastly, I have translations (both finished and planned) that I'll post here!
