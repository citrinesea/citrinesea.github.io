STLYE GUIDE



All translators have different terms of service, and it's on the contributors of this website to respect them. I have created this style guide to abide by different possibilities.

There are two kinds of citations: short-style and long form. Short-style citations are used in-text or beneath quote blocks. Long form citations go under the references list section at the end of the page. Both types are included for each section.

Please follow the guide below.

If YOU are the translator

Follow your own terms of service, and include as much as you feel comfortable.

If you are using EN Music (English vers. of Ensemble Stars Music)

Long-form [Story Name], [!/!!]-Era, EN Music

Short-form [Story Name], (EN Music)

If a translator allows their quotes to be posted, and...

(a.) ... doesn't require asking permission

Long-form [Story Name], [!/!!]-Era, [translator name]

Short-form [Story Name], (translator name)

MIDORI

I had no idea that Hasumi-senpai can be as persistent as Morisawa-senpai… We barely had anything to do with Akatsuki outside of Scroll of the Elements so…

KANATA

fufu. We have a bit of history between us and Akatsuki, after all. perhaps that made us ‘Avoid’ each other.

Submarine (310mc)

Submarine, !!-Era, 310mc

(b.) ... does require asking permission.

Ask for permission directly, then, quote like above.

Long-form [Story Name], [!/!!]-Era, [translator name]

Short-form [Story Name], (translator name)

If a translator does not their quotes to be posted...

You have two options:

(1.) Translate the lines yourself, and...

(a.) If you have not translated the full story:

Long-form [Story Name], [!/!!]-Era, (Full story translations): [ORIGINAL translator name]

Short-form [Story Name], (your name)

This is to encourage readers to see the original work.

KEITO

If he were properly produced, he’d become an interesting idol, wouldn’t he?

Crossroad (citrinesea)

Crossroad, !-Era, (Full story translations): acarnivalofstars, reikeiP

(b.) If you have translated the full story:

Long-form [Story Name], [!/!!]-Era, [your name]

Short-form [Story Name], (your name)

(2) Paraphrase the contents of the quote, and add short-form citation in-text.

When Midori mentions their lack of relationship with AKATSUKI, Kanata suggests it's because of their past (Meteor Impact, 310mc)
