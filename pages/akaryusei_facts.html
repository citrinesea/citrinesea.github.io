This page keeps track of random lore bits about AKARYUSEIbuki, updated and corrected over time.

Can AKARYUSEIbuki cook?

Keito Hasumi
Cooks if he has to
Keito does not recreationally cook often, but can follow a recipe when he needs to. He makes Chinese steamed buns in Chinatown and Valentine's-themed wagashi in Gourmand.
Kuro Kiryu
Yes
Because his father has to work, Kuro takes over the cooking in his family.
Souma Kanzaki
Yes
As explained in Souma Substory 2, Souma specializes in a range of dishes, from Japanese to Western to Chinese and even confectionary. He also specializes in preparing seafood, as seen in A Favor Given, A Favor Received.
Tetora Nagumo
Not well, but learning
Initially, Tetora is bad at cooking, managing to char his attempts or cause fires in kitchens, as seen in Hot & Elegant Chocolat Fes, Sweets Box, and High and Low.

He is currently learning how to cook; he can be seen buying ingredients to prepare seafood in his Stella Maris minitalks and has recently made edible (burnt) pancakes.

Midori Takamine
Cooks if he has to
Midori is able to cook meals if he has to, like in Hot & Elegant Chocolat Fes, Changing The Flavor Up A Bit, and Delicious Happiness.
Shinobu Sengoku
Sweets and snacks
In Hot & Elegant Chocolat Fes, Shinobu says he's made sweets with Souma before so he's quite good at it. He mentions that he buys ingredients to make hyorogan (ninja provisions) in The Place I Finally Reached.
Chiaki Morisawa
Learning
Chiaki starts learning how to cook in I'm At Your Service, Master, where he mentions trying to learn through following recipe books.
Kanata Shinkai
Yes
Kanata specializes in seafood. He does not enjoy eating hot meals, and so prefers cold.
Ibuki Taki
Yes
Ibuki prepares sushi platters for Niki, Rinne, Tatsumi and Jun in Hey Guys, Let's Have Some SUSHI.

Can AKARYUSEIbuki play instruments?

Keito Hasumi
Yes
Keito can play shamisen, as seen in Crossroad and VS AUDIENCE, and acoustic guitar according to the office items. He can also play yokobue (a fife-like flute), as seen when he teaches Wataru in Crash Course.

According to the Oshi Room filter system, he is the only one of AKARYUSEI who is listed as "proficient at a musical instrument."
