Flutter Kick

アッパーカット

Paddle your legs back and forth without letting your knees touch the floor! Get into the rhythm and burn fat!

Knee Extensions

ニーフレックス

Lie on your stomach, lift your knees off the floor, and tap your butt with your heels! Get into the rhythm and burn fat!

Kneeling Push-ups

ニーリングプッシュアップ

Bend and straighten your arms while kneeling to strengthen your arms and chest muscles!

Normal Push-ups

ノーマルプッシュアップ

Do a push-up! This move strengthens your arms and chest muscles!

Reverse Push-ups

リバースプッシュアップ

Sit, lean back, and do a push-up motion while you face forward! This will work your upper arms and shoulders!

Side Lunge Propellers

サイドランジプロペラ

Spread your arms out and twist your body so that you can touch your toes on the opposite side of your hands! Get into the rhythm and burn fat!

Standing Abs

スタンディングアプス

Stand up straight and touch your elbows to your knees! Effective for tightening up your core!

Tricep Extensions

トライセプスキックバック

Work out by leaning forward and lifting your elbows! Focus on putting pressure on your upper arms! Grab a weight or plastic bottle for a more effective workout!

Uppercut

アッパーカット

Swing your arms up from below while squatting, bending and straightening your knees! Follow the rhythm and burn fat!

V-Step

Vステップ

Step in place, and then step forward diagonally, right then left. Repeat. Get into the rhythm and burn fat!
